Our Story

Building the World's Commercial Spaces Since 1984

Hongye Furniture Group Co., Ltd. was founded in 1984 with a single vision: to manufacture commercial furniture of the highest quality, delivered directly to clients without compromise. What began as a regional manufacturer has grown into a global commercial furniture powerhouse.

Today, we operate from a 350,000㎡ state-of-the-art facility in China, serving over 50 countries across 5 continents. Our 2,000+ skilled craftspeople and engineers work daily to translate client visions into spaces that endure.

We serve hotels, office developments, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and luxury residential projects — always with factory-direct pricing and uncompromised quality.

Laser Edge Banding
